## Operator Safety in Extreme Conditions

Working in post-fire zones presents numerous safety hazards:

- Unstable root systems  
- Hidden hot spots  
- Toxic particulates in the air  
- Equipment overheating risks  

Constructors enforced strict protocols:

- Rotating operators every 2 hours  
- On-site cooling tents  
- Continuous air-quality readings  
- Remote telematics monitoring for engine heat and pressure  

These safety measures kept the entire fleet and workforce operational for the full recovery duration.

The **Hino 500 Dump Truck** is a powerful and reliable hauling solution for construction, mining, and large-scale earthmoving operations. Built with a heavy-duty chassis and reinforced dump body, it handles demanding loads with ease and consistency.

Its fuel-efficient engine and robust drivetrain deliver dependable performance across long routes, steep inclines, and rough terrain. Contractors rely on the Hino 500 for efficient material transport and outstanding durability.

## High-Capacity Hauling  
The 20 m³ dump body provides excellent payload capability, allowing crews to move more material per trip. This reduces cycle times and increases productivity across earthwork operations such as grading, excavation, and site development.

The hydraulic dump system ensures smooth and controlled lifting, enabling fast unloading even with compacted or wet material.

### Reinforced Body Structure  
The steel body is engineered to withstand heavy impact and abrasive loads, enhancing service life and reducing maintenance costs.

## Operator Comfort & Control  
The spacious cab provides clear visibility, ergonomic seating, and intuitive controls—supporting long work shifts with minimal fatigue. Advanced braking and traction systems improve safety when navigating difficult terrain or steep gradients.

Digital instrumentation gives operators real-time updates on engine health, fuel usage, and system alerts, helping maintain safe and efficient operation.

### Ideal for Earthmoving & Construction Logistics  
From hauling aggregates to transporting soil and demolition debris, the Hino 500 serves as a dependable fleet asset.

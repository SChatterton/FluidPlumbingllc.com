The **CAT 140K Motor Grader** is designed for precision grading, road construction, and fine earth leveling. Its balanced frame and advanced hydraulics make it ideal for achieving smooth, accurate surfaces on roads, pads, and site preparation areas.

CAT engineering ensures that the 140K delivers excellent controllability and high reliability in demanding conditions. With a powerful engine and responsive blade control system, this grader is a trusted choice for professional contractors.

## Precision Grading Performance  
The 140K features a robust circle drive system and a highly responsive hydraulic blade. Operators can make fine adjustments during grading, enabling smooth finish surfaces with minimal passes.

Its strong moldboard and adjustable articulation allow the machine to adapt to various slope angles, tight turns, and complex grading patterns. This flexibility enhances efficiency and reduces rework.

### Built for Smooth Finish Cuts  
Whether shaping road crowns or leveling large surface areas, the 140K provides excellent consistency.

## Comfortable & Efficient Operation  
The operator station is designed for clear visibility of the moldboard and work area. Ergonomic controls reduce fatigue, while the advanced monitoring system provides real-time diagnostics for optimal performance.

Hydraulic load-sensing technology ensures the grader only uses the power it needs, improving fuel efficiency and precision.

### Ideal for Roads & Large Sites  
From public works to private development, the 140K excels in long, continuous grading operations.

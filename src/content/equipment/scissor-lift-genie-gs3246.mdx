The **Genie GS-3246 Scissor Lift** is a highly efficient electric aerial work platform designed for indoor and tight-space applications. Its compact footprint and zero-emission operation make it ideal for warehouses, malls, factories, and maintenance environments.

The lift provides excellent platform stability, ensuring safe elevation for workers performing electrical, mechanical, and installation tasks.

## Smooth & Precise Lift Performance  
Powered by an electric drive system, the GS-3246 delivers smooth lifting motion and precise control at all working heights. Its proportional joystick offers accurate positioning, essential for work requiring detailed alignment or delicate installation.

The scissor mechanism provides exceptional rigidity, reducing platform sway and increasing worker confidence during elevation.

### Quiet Operation  
Electric motors significantly reduce noise, supporting indoor use without disrupting facility operations.

## Safety & Maneuverability  
The GS-3246 is equipped with multiple safety features including pothole guards, overload sensors, and automatic braking systems. Its non-marking tires and tight turning radius allow easy navigation through narrow aisles and crowded areas.

The platform accommodates two workers with tools, making it suitable for various maintenance and construction tasks where efficiency and safety are priorities.

### Perfect for Indoor Work  
From lighting installation to HVAC servicing, this lift supports safe and productive elevated work environments.

provide rental services, project support, logistics, and operational safety.

- Full name  
- Company name (if applicable)

- Phone number

### 2.2 Improving Client Experience
- Providing accurate recommendations for machinery type

- Offering maintenance and support coordination  

### 2.3 Communication
- Responding to service inquiries  
- Sending booking confirmations and updates  
- Notifying clients about equipment availability or operational changes  

### 2.4 Marketing (Optional)
With your consent, we may send:

- Preventing fraud or misuse of equipment  
- Complying with industry regulations & government requirements

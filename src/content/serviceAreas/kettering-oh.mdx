### Tips to Keep Your Plumbing Working Smoothly in Kettering, OH

In Kettering, OH, seasonal changes and weather can take a toll on your plumbing system. Taking some preventative steps can help you avoid costly repairs and keep everything flowing smoothly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

- **Keep an eye on slow drains:** Don't ignore slow drains — they can be a sign of a bigger clog forming. Regular drain cleaning services can help prevent this.
- **Watch for pipe leaks:** Small leaks can lead to major water damage. Check your pipes regularly for any signs of moisture.
- **Service your water heater:** Regular water heater services, like flushing the tank and checking the pressure valve, will help avoid breakdowns.
- **Prep your pipes for winter:** Insulate your pipes during cold months to avoid freezing and bursting.
